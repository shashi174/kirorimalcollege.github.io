About Us

Kirori Mal College, an institution of academic excellence, established in 1954, that has always strived to, and successfully maintained its place as one of the finest within the University of Delhi. We at Kirori Mal believe in providing for our students an environment rich in knowledge and supportive of their extracurricular interests. The college encourages a quest for knowledge that is rooted in an ethical understanding of the world that we inhabit and this enthusiasm for learning along with a desire to evolve into socially responsible beings is reflected not only in the academic atmosphere but also visible in the field of extra-curricular activity. Our tradition of excellence in theatre, art and music only adds to the richness of the academic fabric of the college.


From the Principal's Desk

Kirori Mal College began as Nirmala College, started by American Jesuits in the wake of Partition. It was located on Qutab Road. Faced with intractable problems related to the staff in the period following the Partition, the management of the College changed hands and the trust founded by Seth Kirori Mal took over the college. The College shifted to its present campus on 1 February 1954. Through the five eventful decades of its history, Kirori Mal College has celebrated the quest for expanding the landscapes of learning. Today, KMC stands for excellence and continuously sets the highest standards. The college is an alma mater which all alumni look back to with fond memories. Our College has given this country many distinguished personalities and leaders in diverse fields.

Kirori Mal is ideal for both academically-inclined students as well as those who are interested in sports and extra curricular activities
