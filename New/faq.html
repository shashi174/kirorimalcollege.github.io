FAQs

Kirori Mal College, an institution of academic excellence, established in 1954, that has always strived to, and successfully maintained its place as one of the finest within the University of Delhi. We at Kirori Mal believe in providing for our students an environment rich in knowledge and supportive of their extracurricular interests. The college encourages a quest for knowledge that is rooted in an ethical understanding of the world that we inhabit and this enthusiasm for learning along with a desire to evolve into socially responsible beings is reflected not only in the academic atmosphere but also visible in the field of extra-curricular activity. Our tradition of excellence in theatre, art and music only adds to the richness of the academic fabric of the college.
